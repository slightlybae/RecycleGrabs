RecycleGrabs is a community oriented organization, with focus on Waste Recycling, Waste Management and Education, Buying and Selling of Recyclables. We motivate households, enterprise and individuals to sell their plastic wastes, and other recyclables to recycling entities and companies.

Over 75% of the land area on earth is degraded. Some 3.2 billion people worldwide are negatively affected by contaminated land. Land pollution has a direct effect in the well being of people. It touches essentially, every area of the world including water pollution, climate change, infertile soil, air pollution.
